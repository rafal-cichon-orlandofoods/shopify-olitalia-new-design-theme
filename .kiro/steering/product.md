# Product Overview

This is **Olitalia**, a premium olive oil e-commerce brand built on Shopify. The theme is called "Combine" (v3.1.1) by KrownThemes.

## Brand Focus
- **Primary Product**: Extra Virgin Olive Oil and Mediterranean food products
- **Target Market**: Premium food enthusiasts and home cooks
- **Brand Values**: Authentic Mediterranean flavor, quality craftsmanship, traditional recipes

## Key Features
- **Recipe Integration**: Extensive recipe system with structured data, chef profiles, and cooking instructions
- **Chef Showcase**: Featured chef profiles (Michele Loffreno, Marco Rossi) with dedicated pages
- **Product Bundling**: Bundle builder and product set functionality
- **Multi-language Support**: Extensive localization (25+ languages)
- **Recipe Submission**: Customer recipe submission forms
- **Educational Content**: Blog posts, cooking tips, and product education

## Business Model
- Direct-to-consumer olive oil sales
- Recipe-driven content marketing
- Chef partnerships and collaborations
- Product education and cooking inspiration
